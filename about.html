About Me

A Quick Intro...

Hi, I'm Brian. Thanks for stopping by to review some of my professional writing samples. I’ve been writing, technically, since 2005. I’ve worked on a number of technical writing projects, from healthcare IT products, to home design software, to a smart building product. I’ve even delivered pizza (which, ironically, was the topic of my first technical writing project while in college).

This site is intended to showcase samples of help topics I’ve written and video tutorials I’ve produced. I've also included some non-technical stuff I've created that, hopefully, gives you a better idea of who I am. sit aspernatur.

Brian Herald MEd.

Skills at a Glance

Throughout my career I've had the opportunity to work with a number of content creation tools and editing tools. Here's a quick look at the tools I'm most experienced with.

About that non-technical side...

I’ve also experimented with some creative writing - I’ve self published four children’s books written by my sons. You can see this work here: Herald and Sons Books.


These projects involved a lot of organization, preparation, and overall project management. They involved working with an illustrator (vetting and hiring the right person, providing detailed sceen descriptions and approving sketches and drafts), formatting and layout for the print and e-books, working with a narrator to produce the audio, and then marketing to position each story properly. Finally, coordinating with the various retailers for selling the audio, e-book, and print versions.
